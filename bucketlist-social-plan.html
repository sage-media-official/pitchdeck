01 Brand Analysis

A community brand with a story worth telling — under-told on social.

Bucketlist sells transformation, not just trips: curated group and private adventures that turn strangers into friends. The product is emotional and highly visual — which makes it perfect for Instagram, and a missed opportunity if the feed only shows destinations.

Category
Curated outbound adventure & experiential travel — licensed Saudi operator (CR 2052103390).
Offer
All-inclusive group trips to curated destinations (Mexico, Cuba, Morocco, Iceland, Norway, China) + private trips (min 4, 1-month notice).
Audience
Adventure-minded Gulf/Saudi adults, 22–40, with disposable income; bilingual (Arabic + English); solo travelers & friend groups.
Proof & trust
2–3 installment payments, full refund at 30+ days, guided & culturally-respectful itineraries; from ~12,000 ﷼.
Tone
Warm, enthusiastic, community-first — "What's on your bucketlist?"

The social opportunity

  • Emotion is the product. Friendships and firsts are more shareable than skylines — we lead with people, not just places.
  • Trust closes travel sales. Installments, refunds, "what's included" and real reviews turn interest into DMs.
  • Reels for reach, carousels for depth. A 50/50 mix compounds discovery and saves — the two metrics that grow a travel account.
  • Bilingual by design. Arabic-first captions with English support widen reach across the Gulf.
  • Consistency beats bursts. A steady 5-a-week rhythm trains the algorithm and the audience.

09 Reel & Carousel Playbooks

How every post is built to perform.

Repeatable structures so quality stays high while we produce at volume.

REEL The reel formula

0–1sHook. A bold line or striking first frame — stop the scroll before they flick.
1–4sSetup. Promise the payoff — the place, the feeling, the tip.
4–15sDeliver. Fast cuts on trending audio; text-on-screen for sound-off viewing.
EndCTA + loop. One clear action; last frame invites a re-watch.
7–21s lengthTrending audioCaptions AR+ENVertical 9:16

CAROUSEL The carousel formula

Slide 1Hook cover. A scroll-stopping promise or bold number.
2–7Value. One idea per slide; skimmable, branded, screenshot-ready.
Swipe cueMomentum. Arrows & open loops pull the swipe (boosts reach).
LastCTA slide. Save, DM, or link — plus a follow prompt.
6–9 slides4:5 portraitSave-worthyBranded template
