01 The Opportunity & Strategic Approach

The sale is decided in two places — the carpenter's hands and the dealer's counter.

A homeowner rarely names a board brand. The carpenter, contractor or architect recommends it, and the dealer stocks what moves. Echon has the product story and the scale — what it needs is a branded, channel-deep go-to-market. The strategy rests on four pillars, and every rupee maps to one.

PILLAR 01

Win the carpenter

The true decision-maker. Nukkad meets, a carpenter loyalty program, samples and recognition make Echon the default recommendation at the point of fabrication.

PILLAR 02

Arm the counter

Dealers & distributors are the revenue engine. Signage, counter branding, samples, meets and trade visibility help the sales team onboard and sell through.

PILLAR 03

Build consumer pull

ATL, always-on social, influencers, the visualizer and experience corners make consumers ask for Echon by name — easing the channel's job.

PILLAR 04

Convert credibility

Translate 22 years and 'India's largest exporter' into domestic trust — through PR, content, AID engagement and project specs, government & private.

03 Target Segments

Three segments, three jobs.

The channel sells, the influencers recommend, the consumer pulls. Marketing serves all three at once.

Segment 1

Channel & Direct Sales

The revenue engine

Dealers & distributors, retailers, and large projects — government and private. The stock points that move volume.

How marketing wins them: trade visibility (signage, counter branding), dealer / contractor meets, loyalty, samples & catalogs, exhibition leads, project-spec support, corporate AV.

Goal: onboard, stock, sell-through
Segment 2

The Influencers

The recommenders

Architects, interior designers, builders / contractors — and, most importantly, carpenters, who specify and fabricate at the point of decision.

How marketing wins them: carpenter nukkad meets + loyalty, AID engagement & CPD, visualizer / 3D plugin, technical content, samples and recognition.

Goal: become the default recommendation
Segment 3

End Consumers

The pull

Homeowners, renovators and self-builders choosing durable, low-maintenance surfaces over wood.

How marketing wins them: ATL, always-on social, influencer content, website + visualizer, performance lead-gen, experience corners and (aggressive plan) a brand ambassador.

Goal: create pull

A credible national Year-1 push — channel, influencer and demand engines fully funded. Recommended.

₹7.90 Cr

Each figure is the all-in investment. The agency fee is 20% of the budget (strategy, execution & monthly reporting); the remaining 80% is working media across the channels below. For Balanced: ₹3.19 Cr media + ₹0.80 Cr agency = ₹3.99 Cr.
