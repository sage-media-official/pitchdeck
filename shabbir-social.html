05 — Running Ads

The same creative, put to work.

Organic builds the credibility; ads put it in front of the right rooms — by job title, industry and geography, not hashtags.

What the ad service covers

  • Campaign build & management on Meta (Instagram + Facebook) and Google, from the month's best creative.
  • Proper targeting — procurement, HSE, ops and site roles across the UAE; retargeting for everyone who engaged.
  • Lead handling — enquiries routed to WhatsApp/email so your sales team responds while interest is hot.
  • Monthly optimisation & reporting — budget moves to what performs; you always see cost per enquiry.

What it needs from you

  • Ad budget, paid directly to the platforms — separate from our fee, always in your control.
  • A workable starting point: AED 3,000–5,000/month — enough for Meta to learn and deliver steady enquiries.
  • Access to your Meta Business Manager and page (we'll set this up with you in week one).
  • A WhatsApp number where leads should land.
Straight with you: the first month of ads is calibration — real enquiry costs replace estimates by month two, and budget follows whatever the numbers say. When you're ready to scale into lead-gen campaigns and Africa distributor outreach, the full growth plan we presented is ready to switch on.
